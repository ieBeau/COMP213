Gyuto Knife
View Larger Picture

For the chef who seeks a single blade that excels in every task, the Hazaki Gyuto knife is the ultimate kitchen tool. Known as Japan's “chef's knife”, this versatile blade is perfect for slicing, chopping, and dicing with remarkable ease. Its longer blade offers a well-balanced design that provides effortless control and precision. The Gyuto is also ideal for those who prefer a traditional 'rocking' technique, making it suitable for various cutting styles. Elevate your culinary skills with the Hazaki Gyuto, where performance meets artistry.

  • Forged: Seki, Japan
  • Blade material: Damascus
  • Handle material: Maple Burl
  • Blade length: 210mm
