The Rainforest Conservatory

Springbrook National Park, Springbrook, Australia. (Waren Brasse/Unsplash) 2020.

Protecting rainforests is essential for biodiversity, climate regulation, and oxygen production. They influence water cycles, provide medicinal resources, support indigenous communities, and offer economic opportunities through sustainable practices. Their preservation is crucial for both environmental health and human well-being.
