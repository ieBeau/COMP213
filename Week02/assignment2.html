Conservation and Environmental Protection

Springbrook National Park, Springbrook, Australia. (Waren Brasse/Unsplash) 2020.

Protected areas (or conservation areas) are are designated regions intended to conserve biodiversity and natural resources. There are many organizations around the world that work to save these areas and the various species that live in them. Below are some examples of such organizations that provide information on the importance of these ecosystems and how to protect them.
